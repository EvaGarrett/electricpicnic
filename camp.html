Camping

General Camping

Entry for Early Entry Ticket Holders is from 4pm on Thursday, 29th August 2019 until 1pm Monday, 2nd September 2019.
There are five campsites that are available to all general camping tickets, you may chose which ever campsite appeals to you most.
You will need to bring your own tent and camping equiptment.

Family Camping

Those wishing to stay in the Family Campsite must be accompanied by children 12 years or younger. No single or groups of adults without children will be admitted entry. The Family campsites are restricted in space and therefore must be reserved solely for families with children. Extended family and relatives with no children must camp in general campsites.Please note that ID checks will be in place at the festival, if you feel your child looks older than 12 please bring ID to back this up (photocopies will suffice).

Boutique Camping

Electric Picnic 2019 has a range of accommodation offerings. The amazing Pink Moon Village will be back for yet another year to please the thousands of Picnic fans with some great structures to go alongside the wonderful facilities which they offer. If it is some of life’s finest luxuries you're after Harvest Moon (in the woods) could be exactly what you need this year, offering a brand new camping experience through some of the finest yurts you’re likely to see. If it’s a comfy lodge or spacious bell tent you would prefer, then the revamped boutique campsite, Harvest Moon Boutique, has just the thing for you.
